Annual Regional Summit: Registration Now Open for All Members

We are pleased to announce that registration for the upcoming 2025 ASATA Regional Summit is officially open. This year's theme, "Synergy in Motion," will focus on the evolving landscape of sports management and community integration.

The summit will feature keynote speakers from leading athletic institutions, workshops on digital transformation in sports governance, and exclusive networking sessions designed to foster collaboration across our member network.

"This summit represents a pivotal moment for our association as we align our strategic goals with the latest advancements in performance technology." — Executive Board

Members are encouraged to secure their spots early as seating is limited. Early-bird registration benefits include priority access to specialized breakout sessions and a digital toolkit for high-performance management.

  • check_circle Full access to all keynote sessions and workshops.
  • check_circle Complimentary networking dinner at the Main Hall.
  • check_circle Certificate of participation for professional development credits.
